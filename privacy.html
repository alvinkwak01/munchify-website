Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026  ·  Effective immediately upon use of Munchify

The short version: Munchify is built to help you eat better — not to monetize your data. We do not sell your information to anyone, ever. We do not show you ads. We collect only what's necessary to make the app work, and you can delete your account and all your data at any time.

1. Who We Are

Munchify is developed and operated by Alvi Kwak, based in Washington State, United States. When this policy says "we," "us," or "our," it refers to Alvi Kwak operating as Munchify.

If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at contact@munchify.app.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide and improve Munchify.

Information you provide directly:

  • Account details — your email address and display name when you sign up
  • Health and body data — height, weight, age, sex, activity level, and goals (e.g. Lose Weight, Build Muscle) that you enter during onboarding
  • Food logs — meals, photos, and nutritional entries you create in the app
  • Water intake — glasses logged per day
  • Pantry items — ingredients you save as staples
  • Community content — dishes and recipes you choose to share publicly

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data — how you interact with the app (screens visited, features used, errors encountered)
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Apple Health data — steps, active calories, and body weight, if you grant permission

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to operate and improve Munchify:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To calculate personalized calorie goals, macro targets, and water intake recommendations based on your body data and goals
  • To power AI-driven features including ingredient scanning, recipe generation, and meal analysis via GPT-4o (OpenAI)
  • To display your nutrition history, trends, and progress
  • To enable community features if you choose to participate
  • To send you relevant notifications about your goals (only if you enable them)
  • To diagnose and fix bugs and improve app performance

We do not use your data to serve you ads. We do not build advertising profiles. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third party.

4. Food Photos and AI Analysis

When you use the Snap feature, your food photo is sent securely to OpenAI's API (GPT-4o) for nutritional analysis. OpenAI processes the image to identify food items and estimate macros. This is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

We do not store your food photos on our servers after analysis is complete. Photos are transmitted, analyzed, and discarded — they are not retained or used to train our own models.

5. Third-Party Services

Munchify integrates with a small number of trusted third-party services to function:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (your account data and meal logs are stored here, encrypted at rest)
  • OpenAI — AI-powered food analysis and recipe generation
  • RevenueCat — subscription and payment management
  • Apple HealthKit — health data sync (only if you grant permission)
  • USDA FoodData Central — public nutritional database used for food search

Each of these services operates under its own privacy policy. We do not share more data with them than is necessary for the feature to function.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase-managed servers with encryption at rest and in transit (TLS). Access to your data is protected by row-level security — your information is only accessible to you and authorized app operations.

While we implement industry-standard safeguards, no online system is completely immune to security risks. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have meaningful control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data directly in the app at any time
  • Correction: Update your profile, goals, and health data in app settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile tab. Deletion is permanent and irreversible
  • Apple Health: Revoke HealthKit permissions at any time via iOS Settings
  • Notifications: Disable push notifications at any time via iOS Settings

To request data export or deletion by email, contact us at contact@munchify.app. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Munchify is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us at contact@munchify.app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by email before the changes take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last revised. Continued use of Munchify after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
